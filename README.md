## üì± Bonus: Reprogram Your NFC Card!

The NFC card you received can be repurposed to link directly to your resources, your digital business card, or any content you want to share instantly with a tap!

### How to Reprogram Your NFC Card

#### **For Android Users:**

**üì± Device Compatibility:**
- ‚úÖ Most Android phones from 2014+ have NFC capability
- ‚úÖ Check: Settings ‚Üí Connected Devices ‚Üí Connection Preferences ‚Üí NFC
- ‚úÖ Enable NFC before using NFC Tools

**Step 1: Download NFC Tools** (Free)
- üì≤ [Google Play Store - NFC Tools](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.wakdev.wdnfc)
- Developer: wakdev
- Rating: 4.5+ stars | 10M+ downloads
- Size: ~8 MB
- No account required, no in-app purchases needed

**Step 2: Prepare Your Content**

Before programming, decide what you want to share:
- üåê Your portfolio website URL
- üìß Your email and contact info (vCard)
- üíº LinkedIn profile link
- üì± Social media profiles

**Step 3: Program Your NFC Card**

1. **Open NFC Tools** app
2. Tap the **"WRITE"** tab at the bottom
3. Tap **"Add a record"** (big blue button)
4. **Choose your record type:**

   **Option A: URL/URI** (Most popular)
   - Select "URL/URI"
   - Enter your full website URL (including https://)
   - Example: `https://yourportfolio.com` or `https://linkedin.com/in/yourname`

   **Option B: Contact Card (vCard)**
   - Select "Contact (vCard)"
   - Fill in your details:
     - Name, phone, email
     - Company/organization
     - Website and social media
   - When someone taps your card, they can instantly save your contact

   **Option C: Multiple Records** (Advanced)
   - Add multiple records to one card
   - Example: URL + Social Media links
   - Tap "Add a record" multiple times

5. **Review your record(s)**
   - Check spelling and links carefully
   - Tap "‚úì" or "OK" to confirm

6. **Write to card:**
   - Tap **"Write"** button (checkmark at top right)
   - You'll see "Ready to scan" message
   - **Hold your NFC card flat against the back of your phone**
   - **Keep it steady for 2-3 seconds**
   - Wait for success confirmation ‚úÖ

7. **Test your card:**
   - Tap "READ" tab
   - Scan your card to verify content
   - Test with another phone if possible

**üîß Troubleshooting:**

**"No NFC tag detected":**
- ‚úÖ Make sure NFC is enabled in phone settings
- ‚úÖ Remove phone case (it may block signal)
- ‚úÖ Try different positions on the back of phone
- ‚úÖ Hold card steady for 3-5 seconds

**"Write failed":**
- ‚úÖ Card may be read-only (check card packaging)
- ‚úÖ Try formatting the card first (Advanced settings in app)
- ‚úÖ Card may be too full (delete old records first)

**"Can't find NFC setting":**
- ‚úÖ Search phone settings for "NFC"
- ‚úÖ May be under: Connections, Wireless, or More Networks
- ‚úÖ Your phone may not support NFC (check specs online)

**üí° Pro Tips:**
- üíæ **Save configurations** - Tap "Save" in app to reuse later
- üîÑ **Erase and rewrite** - Cards can be reprogrammed unlimited times
- üîí **Lock your card** - Prevent accidental overwrites (Advanced settings)
- üì± **Test on multiple phones** - Android and iPhone read differently
- üé® **Add multiple records** - Combine URL + social media links
- üìä **Track scans** - Use link shorteners (bit.ly) to track card taps
